Two Good Beginner Guitars

Squier 40th Anniversary Stratocaster

This is a great beginner electric guitar because it has a comfortable neck, classic Stratocaster sound, and works well for rock, blues, pop, and worship music.

Yamaha Acoustic Electric Guitar

This is a strong beginner acoustic option because it has a warm sound, good playability, and built-in electronics for plugging into an amp.
